Game Projects

My most recent work is on Bewitched, focusing primarily on enemy AI and combat gameplay.

This graph shows a custom pathfinding graph where each sphere represents an abstract point and the color represents the cost. This is what is used to direct enemies around each other and towards the player.

Attacks create much costlier regions on the graph that other enemies avoid. Larger enemies have a higher priority and therefore attack more often and take more direct paths to the player.
